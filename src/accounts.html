Explore New Artists

My art is all over the place but I like to try and explore everything. From painting to sculpting to metal working or 3D printing I'm interested in it all. I want to create a large skill set for myself so when I have an idea I can create every aspect of that project. I like to do a lot of digital designing with 3D softwares and create those ideas into physical objects, or use different technologies to enhance my art. I used to hate technology and was overwhelmed by it, now I use some sort of tech in almost all my art pieces. Whether it's for planning a construction, modeling it, creating the overall image, or creating extra pieces for the artwork, some sort of tech is involved. I also have a very specific character design I like to work with in hopes of creating a brand with it. In the future I want to print on clothing and other fabrics to see how my ideas translate over to wearable items.
